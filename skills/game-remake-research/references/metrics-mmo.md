# MMO Metrics

Use this reference when the primary lens is MMO and you need quantitative baselines instead of vague judgments.

## Core Metrics

| Metric | Unit | How to capture | Why it matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Daily route completion time | `minutes/session` | Time a representative mandatory daily route from login to completion. | Measures daily burden and fatigue risk. |
| Time to first mandatory group gate | `hours-to-unlock` | Record when solo progress first meaningfully stalls without party, guild, or raid access. | Shows how dependent the game is on social progression. |
| Mandatory sink pressure | `percent-of-gross-currency` | Estimate required sinks as a share of gross currency earned in a representative progression band. | Reveals economy durability and inflation control. |
| Market dependency for progression-critical upgrades | `percent-of-upgrades` | Sample how many meaningful upgrades are realistically sourced through trade or services. | Critical when remaking the game without a live market. |
| Major reset cadence | `days/reset` | Record daily, weekly, and seasonal reset intervals tied to progression. | Defines the service rhythm the remake must preserve or replace. |

## Interpretation Guardrails

- Compare the same region, platform, and date window.
- Separate solo path numbers from party-optimized numbers.
- Separate launch-phase pacing from mature-endgame pacing.
- Do not infer healthy economy from headline currency drops alone; measure sinks too.
