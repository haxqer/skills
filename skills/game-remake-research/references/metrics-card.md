# Card Metrics

Use this reference when the primary lens is card game and you need rules clarity, curve pacing, and meta health in measurable terms.

## Core Metrics

| Metric | Unit | How to capture | Why it matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Median turn duration | `seconds/turn` | Sample representative early, mid, and late-game turns across multiple matches. | Defines readability pressure and match flow. |
| Turns to primary resource cap or decisive power turn | `turns` | Record when the game reaches full resource availability or a consistent game-ending tempo point. | Maps pacing and comeback bandwidth. |
| Mulligan flexibility | `cards-or-redraw-options` | Document how many cards or choices the opening hand system allows the player to smooth. | Directly affects non-games and opening variance. |
| Top archetype share | `percent-of-sample` | Estimate the share of observed matches or top lists occupied by the leading archetype. | Provides a baseline for matchup diversity and meta concentration. |
| Starter deck performance gap | `estimated-winrate-gap` | Estimate the performance gap between onboarding decks and the live field or intended baseline opponents. | Shows whether onboarding supports or repels new players. |

## Interpretation Guardrails

- Measure ladder and curated event formats separately.
- Track turn length with board complexity, not just clock time.
- Do not confuse archetype popularity with archetype health.
- Separate collection friction from match-rule health.
